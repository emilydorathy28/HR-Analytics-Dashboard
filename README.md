## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built using Microsoft Excel to analyze employee demographics, attrition trends, salary distribution, and workforce insights. The dashboard enables dynamic filtering through slicers and presents key HR metrics using KPI cards and pivot charts.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition across departments.
- Monitor workforce demographics.
- Compare salary across job roles.
- Evaluate work-life balance and job satisfaction.
- Provide interactive insights using slicers.

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Employees
- Attrition Rate
- Average Monthly Income
- Average Age
- Average Experience
- Average Job Satisfaction

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- Department Headcount
- Gender Distribution
- Attrition by Department
- Average Monthly Income by Job Role
- Age Group Distribution
- Overtime vs Attrition
- Education Field Distribution
- Work-Life Balance
- Business Travel Distribution
- Years at Company vs Job Satisfaction

---

## 💡 Business Insights

- Research & Development has the largest workforce.
- Employees working overtime show higher attrition.
- Most employees belong to the 30–39 age group.
- Life Sciences is the most common education field.
- Salary varies significantly across job roles.
